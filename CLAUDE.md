# Orozco Homes — Estimation Rules (2026+)

## Location & Timeline Context
- **Primary Market:** Southeast United States (Baseline: Virginia Beach / Hampton Roads, VA)
- **Target Pricing Horizon:** Calendar Year 2026 and forward (including 2026 inflationary indexes)

## Mandatory Remodeling Scopes
All estimation features, database seed data, and calculation logics must reflect 2026 local pricing for:
1. **Bathroom Renovations** (Plumbing, tiling, fixtures)
2. **Kitchen Renovations** (Cabinetry, stone countertops, appliances)
3. **Additions** (Footings, framing, foundation tie-ins)
4. **Porticos** (Structural columns, roofing, entry masonry)
5. **Garage Conversions** (Slab prep, insulation packages, HVAC mini-splits)
6. **Attic Conversions** (Staircase egress, joist reinforcing, insulation)

## Labor & Material Parameters
- **Trades & Subcontracting:** Base all trade labor rates (Framing, Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC, Drywall, Finish Carpentry) on 2026 regional wage standards.
- **Material Sourcing:** Index all material rates (lumber, drywall, concrete, finishes) to wholesale suppliers and freight matrices serving the Virginia Beach region.
